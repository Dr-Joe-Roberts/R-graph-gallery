Waffle




A Waffle displays categorical data as a collection of small squares, similar to a waffle. Each category is represented by a distinct color of squares, with the total number of squares correlating to the total data count.

For an R implementation, see below.

Step by step - the waffle package

The waffle package and its waffle() function is probably the easiest way to build waffle charts in R. The examples below will teach you how to build a simple waffle chart in just a few lines of code.
